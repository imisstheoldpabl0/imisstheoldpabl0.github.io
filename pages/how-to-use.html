How to use

Welcome to Trade BoyAI: Your Partner in Automated Trading

Congratulations on choosing Trade BoyAI to enhance your trading experience and maximize your profits effortlessly. This guide will walk you through the simple steps to harness the power of our cutting-edge AI trading platform.

1. Sign Up and Log In

  • Visit our website and sign up for an account using your email address.
  • Once registered, log in to access the Trade BoyAI dashboard.

2. Profile Setup

  • Complete your profile by providing necessary information such as your name, contact details, and preferred payment method for transactions.

3.Connect your Exchange Account

  • Connect your existing exchange account (e.g., Binance, Coinbase) to Trade BoyAI securely through API integration. This allows Trade BoyAI to access your trading data and execute trades on your behalf.

4. Set Your Preferences

  • Customize your trading preferences based on your risk tolerance, investment goals, and trading strategies.
  • Choose from various trading strategies offered by Trade BoyAI, such as trend following, arbitrage, or sentiment analysis, to align with your investment style.

5. Deposit Funds

  • Deposit funds into your connected exchange account to ensure Trade BoyAI has the necessary capital for trading activities.
  • You can set predefined limits or rules for fund allocation and withdrawal within the Trade BoyAI platform.

6. Enjoy Hands-Free Trading

  • Sit back, relax, and let Trade BoyAI handle the complexities of trading on your behalf.
  • Enjoy the convenience of automated trading while you focus on other priorities knowing that Trade BoyAI is diligently working to grow your wealth.
